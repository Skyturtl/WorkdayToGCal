Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Summary

This site does not collect or store your calendar contents, class schedule details, event titles, locations, times, or personal identifiers. All parsing happens locally in your browser; only create calls you initiate are sent to Google Calendar. Read‑access scopes bundled by Google are not used.

Open Source Transparency

Source code: GitHub Repository.

Anonymous Analytics

Google Analytics (GA4) gathers minimal, anonymous usage metrics to improve reliability. IP anonymization is enabled. No calendar IDs, titles, or schedule contents are sent.

Collected events:

  • file_imported – increment only.
  • event_created – increment only.
  • session_summary – once per session containing counters & duration.

Parameters: session duration (seconds), counts of events created & files imported, browser user agent. No unique identifiers are added beyond GA’s anonymous cookie.

Cookies

GA4 sets a cookie to distinguish anonymous sessions. You may block or clear it using browser settings; the app still works.

User Control & Opt‑Out

Use tracking protection, clear/block cookies, or install Google’s opt‑out add‑on to disable analytics. Future in‑app opt‑out may be added.
